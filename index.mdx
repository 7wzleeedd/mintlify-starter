<Steps>
  <Step title="bot perms">
    <p>
    before adding miko to your server, ensure you have the following permissions:</p>

    <ul>
    <li>
    Administrator permission or equivalent permissions</li>

    
    <li>
    Bot role must be in the top 5 roles of your server</li>

    
    <li>
    Manage Channels permission for voice features</li>

    
    <li>
    Manage Roles permission for moderation features</li>

    </ul>
  </Step>
  <Step title="add miko to tour Server">
    <p>
    click here to add miko to your server</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="basic setup">
    <p>
    after adding miko, run these essential setup commands:</p>

    <CodeGroup>

    ```
    ,setupCreates a jail channel, log channel, and jail role for moderation
    ```

    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Prefix">
    Greed's default prefix is <code>
    ,</code>

    . To change it, use:

    ```bash
    ,prefix <new_prefix>
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
