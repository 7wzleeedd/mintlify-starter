early access users play a crucial role in shaping the future of bajo by providing valuable feedback and testing new features.

## how to join

1. **join our discord**
   - visit [discord.gg/greedbot](https://discord.gg/greedbot)
   - navigate to the #ticket channel
   - open a ticket and provide your Server ID
2. **apply for access**
   - fill out the application form
   - tell us about your server and how you plan to use the dashboard
   - wait for our team to review your application
3. **get started**
   - once approved, you'll receive some instructions
   - you must first run `,beta enable` in the whitelisted guild to enable access to the bot
   - Join our feedback channel

## early access benefits

### priority features

- access to beta features before public release
- direct influence on feature development
- early bug fixes and updates

### community perks

- special role in our discord server
- access to exclusive channels
- direct communication with developers

### support

- priority support tickets
- dedicated feedback channel
- regular updates on development progress

## feedback & support

as an early access user, your feedback is invaluable. we provide several channels for you to share your thoughts:

- **#your-ticket-channel**: We will keep your ticket open during the beta testing period
- **Direct Messages**: Contact our team for urgent matters

<Note>
  Early access users are expected to provide regular feedback and report any issues they encounter. This helps us improve the dashboard for the public release.
</Note>
